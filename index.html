About This Site

Hope and Ian decided to make a bunch of home made Christmas gifts this year. This site will provide some awesome tips/recipes etc. regarding how to use the gifts that you have received. It also contains some family updates and some pictures. It is updated every year to reflect what is happening each Christmas.

Click your name in the header to see what your presents are. Don't worry, if it's not Christmas yet you won't see your presents. We're better web developers than that ;)
